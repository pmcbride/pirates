Note: Kenney's pack does not include a dedicated lighthouse asset. If we
ship the stock variant for Foglight Cove, we either accept the more generic
"island coastline" framing or composite a hand-drawn lighthouse stamp on
top in MissionScene. Other CC0 lighthouse-specific backgrounds searched
(OpenGameArt "CC0 Backgrounds" set, Wikimedia Commons paintings, Pixabay)
were either wrong style (4-color pixel art), wrong license (Pixabay Content
License, not CC0/CC-BY), or didn't match the warm sticker palette.
