# Foglight Cove background prompts

Asset B — board background for `tutorial-cove`. ~1024×512 wide, sits behind
the 6×3 grid in `MissionScene`. Should evoke "early morning calm sea near a
small lighthouse" without competing with the foreground tile sprites.

## Variant 1 — keeper

```
Children's-book illustration of a calm pale-blue cove at sunrise, gentle
ripples on the water, low-tide rocks scattered on the left edge, a small
white-and-red striped lighthouse standing on a grassy cliff on the right,
soft cream and peach sky with a pale yellow sun low on the horizon. Warm
painterly cartoon style matching a pirate children's coding game. Top-down
isometric perspective for use as a game-board background. No characters,
no ships, no UI, no text, no logos. Composition is uniform and uncrowded
so foreground tile icons read clearly on top. Palette: sea blue #4ec3df,
deep sea #1d6f9f, sky cream #ffe9b8, sun #ffb24a, lighthouse red #ff6b5c,
parchment cream #fff1cf, ink outline #2b1d0e.
```

Why: the explicit "composition is uniform and uncrowded so foreground tile
icons read clearly on top" line was the unlock — earlier attempts were
beautiful but too busy in the center, fighting the tile sprites.

## Variant 2 — alternate (discarded)

```
Wide cove scene at dawn for a pirate coding game, lighthouse on a rocky
outcrop, painted cartoon style, no people, no ships, top-down view, warm
sunset palette, fits a 1024 by 512 background slot under a game grid.
```

Why discarded: too short, model placed a giant lighthouse in the dead center.
Kept Variant 1.
